My Rube Goldberg Machine


My Rube Goldberg machine was designed to chop down a tree. Not a real tree, a Lego tree. It has seven steps:

\
  • 1. A marble catapult is fired by a human.
  • 2. The marble rolls down a track.
  • 3. The marble knocks down Lego minifugure dominoes.
  • 4. The last minifigure pulls an obstacle with a piece of string.
  • 5. A car is free to go down the track and triggures a Lego wrecking ball.
  • 6. The Lego wrecking ball hits a white marble.
  • 7. The white marble rolls down a platform and knocks over a Lego tree.
