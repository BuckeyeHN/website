Who is Rube Goldberg?


Rube Goldberg was a cartoonist who grew up in the United States of America. He also was a sculptor, author, engineer, and inventor. What he was most known for was his strange designs for simple tasks such as turning on a light, or dusting a piece of furniture. His disigns always were incredibly complex and included many steps. To this day, many people are still following in Rube Goldberg's footsteps and enjoy making these designs to sho other people of their skills in physics and science.
