What is a Rube Goldberg machine?


A Rube Goldberg machine is supposed to be a very complicated machine that goes through various steps of intricate designs just to accomplish a simple task such as pushing a button or shooting a basket. There are no rules about how many steps you need or hat the steps need to include. The one rule is to get creative. See what you can do with the things in your own house and make a machine. It doesn't have to be perfect or work every time. Check out thepictures belo to see what some people have done with what you could call useless in a machine. You can use anything! Paper cups, cloths pins, pickles, even live animals!(Maybe...)
