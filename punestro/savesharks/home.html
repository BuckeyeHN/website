Why Should I save sharks?

Sharks are very important creatures to the ecosystem and if they are all gone, many fish that are prey of sharks will grow to a very imbalanced number. Also, when there is an increase in the number of a population, it means more food is consumed. This would start a chain reaction of starvation, overpopulation, and ultimately, mass extinction.
